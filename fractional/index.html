How the engagement works

Every Fractional engagement follows the same five steps, whether you start at Heavy month or Slow Lane.

01Discovery call

30-45 min · free

We figure out scope, fit, and the right starting intensity. Not every business needs Heavy month. Some start at Active build, some only want Slow Lane.

02Intake

~20 min · conversational AI

You walk an AI agent through your business: content state, outbound, production capacity, team structure, ICP. I prep the Blueprint from your answers.

03Kickoff + audit walkthrough

Week 1 · 60-75 min

Combined call. I walk the 90-Day Blueprint audit findings, you push back where they don't match reality, we lock the first month's scope and shipping order.

Priority systems go live. Voice profile trained. Content engine drafting daily. You self-edit copy through the in-browser editor, so there's no Ivan dependency for text changes.

Common questions

Can I start at $6,500 instead of $10,000?

Yes. Heavy month is recommended for first months with a new partner because it covers the audit + 2-3 priority builds. But if your scope is smaller or you already know what you want built, Active build is a fine starting point.

What happens after the first few months?

Most clients move to Slow Lane ($3,500/mo) or the optional Care Plan as the heavy build queue empties. You can also stay at Active build indefinitely.

Can I switch tiers month-to-month?

Yes. Think of the tiers as intensity dials you can turn up or down, with no contract locking you in. We recalibrate monthly based on what's actually needed. Step up if a big build is coming, step down when things settle.

What if I just want one thing built, like a lead magnet system?

That's a Single Project at $7,500 (see /lead-magnet-system). No engagement, no audit, you own the build. Single Project works best when you know exactly what you want and don't need strategic input.

What's the 90-Day Blueprint?

The strategic audit + roadmap I deliver in your first Heavy month. Includes a business + system map, 5-7 priority builds with costed gaps, a 3-phase 90-day plan, and quick wins. It's yours regardless of whether you continue past month 1.

Refund policy?

Month-to-month. If month 1 ships and you don't see value, no continuation. The audit is yours regardless. I'd rather lose a month's revenue than have you stuck in a tier that isn't earning it.
