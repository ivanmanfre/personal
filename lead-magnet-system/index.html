Three weeks isn't a deadline — it's an absorption budget. The bottleneck on lead-magnet systems isn't the build, it's whether your team can field the leads they generate. We ship the magnet only when your CRM, nurture sequence, and qualifying logic are ready to catch them. No firehose into a funnel that can't drink.
